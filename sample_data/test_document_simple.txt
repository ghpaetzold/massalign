April is the fourth month of the year .
It has 30 days .

The name April comes from that Latin word aperire which means `` to open '' .
This probably refers to growing plants in spring .

April begins on the same day of week as July in all years and also January in leap years .

Poets use April to mean the end of winter .
For example : April showers bring May flowers .

April 's flower is the Sweet Pea .
Its birthstone is the diamond .
The meaning of the diamond is innocence .
